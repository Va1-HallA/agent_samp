Fall risk assessment: age 65+, prior fall history, on sedatives/antihypertensives, or with visual impairment are high risk.

Prevention:
1. Bed height 40-50 cm with bedside rails.
2. Night light kept on at night; handrails and non-slip mats in the bathroom.
3. Non-slip shoes; avoid trailing trouser hems.
4. Caregiver-assisted movement within 30 minutes of taking medication.

Post-fall management:
1. Do not move the resident immediately; first assess consciousness, pain location, and mobility.
2. If fracture or head impact is suspected, notify physician immediately and keep resident in place pending assessment.
3. Record fall time, location, activity at the time, and vital signs.
